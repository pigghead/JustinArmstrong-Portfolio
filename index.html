About Me

I have been developing for games as early as 13 years old on Roblox. Although I failed in creating popular games on the platform, I cherish that this was my first experience in scripting and coding. I loved the challenge and the sense of accomplishment whenever I would solve a problem.

Fast forward to today, I am a student at the Rochester Institute of Technology, and I have from simply a coder to a problem solver. Much like when I was younger, I still treat programming as a problem-solving exercise and this perspective has benefitted me immensely.

On this website you will find some of the projects I have worked on throughout college. Some of these are classwork, and others are projects where I wanted to practice or show off what I am learning. Feel free to check out my Github for more samples of my work.
